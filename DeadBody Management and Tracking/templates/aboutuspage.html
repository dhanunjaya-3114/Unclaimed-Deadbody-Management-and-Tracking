About This Project

Purpose

This application is built for dead body management and tracking. It helps authorized staff register unidentified bodies, maintain descriptive records, track status updates, and manage claim verification in a structured workflow.

What The System Handles

The platform supports tag generation, searchable records, claim request submission by the public, claim approval or rejection by authorities, and final status transitions such as claimed or cremated. This improves traceability and reduces manual record mismatch.

Users and Responsibility

Admin, Nurse, and Police roles can register and process records based on permissions. Public users can search records and submit claims with relationship details for review. Every step is tracked to maintain accountability.
